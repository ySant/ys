Introduction

I was born and grew up in Java, Indonesia. Then, I came to the U.S. to study physics, at Texas A&M University. After graduation I did research at several institutions in several countries. I eventually moved to Canada and live there. I am interested in working with computers, and have been studying this field for few years.

Computer Science & Technology

My passion with computers lead me to take a computer technology program at Camosun College, where I learned about computer networking and administration. Later on, I was fortunate to get an opportunity to continue my study further. Now, I am studying computer science at the University of Victoria, working toward my master degree. My interests include algorithms, data analysis, artificial intelligence and computer animation.

Physics

My research topic in physics is dark matter. Dark matter is an unknown matter that was hypothesized to account for discrepancy between the observed matter and the large scale dynamics in the Universe (based on the gravitational laws as we know it). It is an overlapping research area of elementary particle physics, astrophysics, and cosmology. My papers and publications can be found in the Hep Inspires.
